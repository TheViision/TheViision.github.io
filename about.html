ABOUT

Stop motion animation is a captivating and versatile filmmaking technique that has been captivating audiences for over a century. At its core, stop motion involves capturing a series of still images, or frames, and stringing them together to create the illusion of movement. The process is meticulous, requiring patience, attention to detail, and a keen eye for storytelling. The magic of stop motion lies in its simplicity and yet its ability to bring inanimate objects to life. Unlike traditional animation techniques that involve drawing or computer-generated imagery (CGI), stop motion animators work with physical objects, manipulating them frame by frame to create movement
